Frequently asked

Everything about GEO, AEO and why your brand needs it.

What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) and why does it matter?
GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your brand so it appears and is cited when generative AI engines like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity or Gemini answer questions for their users. As more people ask AI before they search Google, GEO has become the new SEO for brands that want to keep being discovered.
How is GEO different from traditional SEO?
Classic SEO optimizes for appearing in Google search results. GEO optimizes for appearing inside the answers generated by AI engines. In GEO you don't compete for a position in a list of blue links; you compete to be cited as a recommendation inside a generated text. Both coexist and reinforce each other, and the signals that improve one tend to improve the other.
How exactly does Argus work?
Argus is a service, not a platform. Each week I manually run the real queries your customers would ask in ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity and Gemini about your sector. I identify where you appear, where you don't, which competitors are taking your place and what sources the AI cites. Every Monday you get an interpreted PDF in your email with three concrete actions for that week. No dashboard, no login, nothing to learn.
Does Argus work for non-Spanish markets?
Yes. Argus has a specialty in Spanish-language markets because that's where the founder operates, but the methodology applies to any language and any market. The first pilot clients include businesses in the United States. If you operate primarily in English, queries are run in English with regional adjustments (US, UK, Canada).
When will I see results after starting GEO work?
Changes in how the AIs cite you typically appear between 2 and 6 weeks after implementing improvements (publishing content in sources the AIs use, improving presence on review sites, getting cited in industry media). Argus measures the evolution week by week so you can see exactly which actions are moving the needle.
Do I need to drop my current SEO work to do GEO?
No. SEO and GEO are complementary. The same signals that improve your SEO (authority, quality content, citations in media) also improve your GEO because the AIs read and learn from the same sites Google indexes. Argus identifies actions with double leverage: they improve both your Google ranking and your AI visibility.
